# Buying Signals — [Client Name]

## Strong signals (high intent, recent)
- Funding announcement in last 6 months
- Leadership hire in target function (VP, Director, C-suite)
- Multiple open roles in target function
- Expansion announcement (new market, new product, new office)
- Public statement about the problem we solve

## Weak signals (lower confidence)
- Generic blog content about the category
- Funding older than 18 months
- Job postings unrelated to target function
- Press coverage without growth indicators

## Disqualifying signals (automatic deprioritise)
- Company is in acquisition or merger process
- Mass layoffs announced in last 90 days
- Leadership team is entirely new (unstable buying environment)

## Evidence requirement
Every signal must include:
- A source URL
- A date or relative time (e.g. "posted 3 weeks ago")

No URL = signal does not count.
Signals older than 6 months = label OLD and reduce ICP score by 1.
