## Voice and tone

### This is how we write
- Direct, no filler
- Specific over vague — numbers, names, real situations
- Peer-to-peer, not vendor-to-buyer
- Short sentences. One idea per line where possible.

### Examples of good sentences
- "[Specific observation about their company] made me think of you."
- "We helped [similar company] do [X] in [timeframe]."
- "Worth a 15-minute call if [specific condition] is on your radar."

### Examples of bad sentences (never write these)
- "I hope this email finds you well."
- "I wanted to reach out because..."
- "We are a leading provider of..."
- "I'd love to connect and explore synergies."

## Sequence rules
- Email 1: research-led, specific signal, one ask
- Email 2: different angle, add proof point, same ask
- Email 3: pattern interrupt or close the loop
- Maximum 3 emails per sequence unless re-engagement trigger fires
- Minimum 4 days between emails
